About me

I am currently a first-year PhD Student in Astrophysics at the Institute of Science and Technology Austria with Prof. Lisa Bugnet, investigating the evolution and structure of magnetic fields of different evolutionary stages of low- and intermediate-mass stars.

Magnetic Fields across the evolution stars

Magnetic fields have been often ignored in the evolution of stars. However, they are expected to significantly alter the mixing of elements and angular momentum in the interior of stars, and thus, may lead to drastically different lifetimes and age estimates across stellar evolution.

By combining asteroseismic methods with stellar evolution codes I will be able to draw connections between the magnetic fields at different stellar evolution stages. I have already investigated the effect of different magnetic field structures on their asteroseismic signature and showed that studying the dipolar oscillation modes will be crucial to disentangling the magnetic structure of magnetic fields in the cores of Red Giant stars.

Currently, I am studying the evolution of magnetic fields in white dwarfs.
